What is Glide Client?

GlideClient is a fork of Soar that aims to fix many of the issues people faced with Soar Client.

It was started by two of the Mods at Soar Client, it was originally planned as a Soar Client update.

We dont want to be competiton with Soar Client as we fully support EldoDebug, therefore when Soar Client 8 releases we will discontinue glide
