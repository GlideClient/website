What is GlideClient?

GlideClient is an enhanced fork of SoarClient that addresses many issues users experienced with SoarClient 7. Our goal is to create one of the most unique Minecraft 1.8.9 PvP clients available, offering distinctive user experiences you won't find elsewhere.

Originally started by two SoarClient moderators, GlideClient began as a planned update for SoarClient. When Soar became open-source, we decided to create a separate project to implement features that wouldn't fit the original SoarClient aesthetic and vision.

Join our growing community of Minecraf 1.8.9 PvP enthusiasts and experience the difference with GlideClient's enhanced design, fixes, and unique features.
