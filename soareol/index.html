SoarClient 7 is now Discontinued

SoarClient is changing its focus towards the next generation of SoarClient which means SoarClient 7 has been discontinued.

People are constantly asking the same questions over and over in the Soar Discord server such as "How do I download?", "Why is the SoarClient site down?", "is SoarClient dead?" and the simple answer to all of those is you need to use the Offline Soar versions like SoarClient Legacy or GlideClient while EldoDebug works on the version eight of SoarClient.

Everyone in the SoarClient and GlideClient Discord servers will be notified when they can get version 8 of SoarClient but we would also love if you subscribed to @eldodebug on YouTube!

....But for now you have 2 Options to choose from to continue using SoarClient

GlideClient

GlideClient is a fork of Soar by several of the staff members of SoarClient and it is still being maintained, support for it is still available too. GlideClient has all the features you like about soar and more!

Get GlideClient

SoarClient Legacy/Offline is SoarClient as it was when v7 was abandoned so it is out of date and is not being maintained, you can still choose to use it but any bugs found will not be fixed

Get SoarClient Legacy (Not Recommended)
